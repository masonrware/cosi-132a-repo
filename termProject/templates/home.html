On each review page, you will see a sentiment analysis score. Sentiment analysis, or opinion mining, is a technique in natural language processing used to automatically determine whether a piece of text is positive, negative, and sometimes neutral. RealView's machine learning algorithms analyze the sentiment of all reviews for a movie and create a composite score for each movie. In addition, our patented AspectExtractor analyzes each review's opinion on specific aspects of movies, such as plot, setting, costume design, and animation style. Our sentiment analysis makes it easier for the user to understand the overall opinion on a movie!
